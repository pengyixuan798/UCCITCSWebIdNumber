Yuan Longping — The Father of Hybrid Rice

Yuan Longping (1930–2021) was a pioneering Chinese agronomist whose development of hybrid rice helped feed hundreds of millions of people. His lifelong mission was to reduce hunger through science, innovation, and dedication to farmers around the world.

Yuan Longping in 2006, holding hybrid rice plants that helped transform global agriculture.

Milestones

  • 1930
    Born in Beijing, China. His childhood across various provinces exposed him to the hardships of agricultural life and planted the seeds of his future research.
  • 1953
    Graduated from Southwest Agricultural College and became an agronomy teacher, beginning his lifelong commitment to improving food production.
  • 1964
    Discovered a naturally occurring male-sterile rice plant, inspiring him to pursue hybrid rice research despite widespread skepticism in the scientific community.
  • 1973
    Successfully developed the world’s first high-yield hybrid rice variety, increasing rice production by at least 20% and marking a historic scientific breakthrough.
  • 1976
    China officially launched the National Hybrid Rice Program led by Yuan Longping, rapidly spreading hybrid rice techniques throughout the country.
  • 1990s
    Advanced the “Super Hybrid Rice” project, pushing rice yields to new global records and significantly improving food security.
  • 2004
    Received the World Food Prize, recognizing his unmatched contributions to fighting global hunger.
  • 2011
    Promoted hybrid rice internationally, helping countries across Asia, Africa, and the Americas adopt high-yield varieties.
  • 2018
    Led breakthroughs in salt-tolerant rice, enabling crop growth in coastal and saline soils and opening new possibilities for global food production.
  • 2019
    Awarded the Order of the Republic, China’s highest honor. His research continued to influence food security worldwide.
  • 2021
    Passed away at age 91. His innovations continue to feed hundreds of millions every year—leaving a legacy that transformed world agriculture.

Famous Quote

“My greatest wish is to see everyone free from hunger.”
— Yuan Longping
